CrewWho runs the paperwork.

Your call sheets become your career.

Freelance crew live and die by call sheets — then throw them away. The numbers you need are buried in PDFs, your days worked are unprovable, and your network lives in a shoebox.

CrewWho reads every call sheet you're sent and turns it into phone contacts, your day's schedule and logistics, GPS-backed timecards, one-tap invoices, and a verifiable history of every job and every person you've worked with — on your phone, private by default, working even with no signal.

What it does

  • Call sheet → contacts in seconds. Photo or share a call sheet; the whole crew lands in your phone with names, roles, and numbers — even messy, scanned, or numbers-withheld sheets.
  • Your shoot day, at a glance. Call-time countdown, location, weather, nearest hospital, wheels-up — on your lock screen, watch, and widgets.
  • Money handled. Day rate + overtime computed at wrap, GPS mileage, receipt scans, and a professional invoice from one tap.
  • Proof of your work. Documented working days — call sheets on file, GPS-corroborated hours — exportable for union applications, visas, or income verification.
  • A career that compounds. Every job, every collaborator, every city on a map; résumé and credits built live from your history; a digital business card with a verified handshake.
  • Private by design. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. Your data stays on your device. Bring your own AI or run it fully on-device.
