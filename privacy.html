CrewWho — Privacy Policy

Provided by James Peterson Films, LLC (“we,” “us,” the “Developer”). Last updated: July 1, 2026.

The short version

CrewWho is built to be private by design. Your call sheets, contacts, notes, ratings, photos, receipts, and location stay on your device. We do not operate servers that receive your data, we do not have accounts, we do not run analytics or advertising, and we never sell your data. Data leaves your device only when you choose to send it — by sharing/exporting it, or by turning on an optional third-party integration under your own account.

Information we do NOT collect

We do not collect, receive, or store your content on our systems. We have no user accounts, no login, no advertising identifiers, and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not build profiles of you and we do not sell or rent any information.

Information processed on your device

The following is created and stored locally on your device (and in any backup you choose to make): scanned/photographed/imported call sheets and the information extracted from them (names, roles, phone numbers, emails, dates, call times, locations, hotels, etc.); your private notes, ratings, tags, availability, and reviews; contact photos; scanned receipts and expense details; timecard entries and mileage; and app settings. This information is used only to provide the app’s features on your device.

Health data (HealthKit)

If you turn on Health corroboration, CrewWho reads step count, active energy, and heart rate from Apple Health, only on your device, solely to estimate your on-set activity window as a cross-check for load-in/out times. In accordance with Apple’s requirements: we do not use HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or any use-based data mining, and we never share, sell, or disclose it to any third party. Health data is used only while the app is open and is not transmitted off your device by CrewWho. You can revoke access anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

Location & motion data

If you enable location or drive detection, CrewWho uses your location and motion data on your device to show local weather, estimate drive mileage, and suggest portal-to-portal in/out times. This data is stored only on your device and is not uploaded or shared by us. For reverse-geocoding of your stops and for weather, only approximate coordinates are sent to the third-party services named below. Location features are off until you turn them on, and you can disable them anytime in iOS Settings.

Optional third-party services you enable

Some features work by connecting to third-party services under your own account or API key. Data is sent to them only when you enable the feature, and each service’s handling of your data is governed by its own privacy policy and terms, which are its responsibility and may change. Please review them before enabling a feature. These include:

  • AI providers you choose (e.g. OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, or others) — receive the call-sheet content you ask them to process, under your API key and their terms.
  • Open-Meteo — receives approximate coordinates to return a weather forecast.
  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — receives approximate coordinates of your stops to return a place name.
  • Google (if you connect it) — Calendar and read-only Gmail, under your Google account.
  • Microsoft (if you connect it) — Outlook read-only mail and calendar, under your Microsoft account.

We are not responsible for third-party services and do not control how they use data you send them.

How your information is shared

We do not share your information with anyone. Any sharing happens only when you initiate it — for example, exporting contacts/receipts/timecards, sending a text or email, or saving to Files/iCloud/Drive/Dropbox. Those actions use your device and accounts and are subject to the terms of the services you send to.

Data retention & deletion

Because your data lives on your device, you control it. You can delete individual items in the app, and deleting the app removes its data from your device. Backups you create and store are under your control to keep or delete.

Security

Your information is protected by your device’s own security (passcode, Face/Touch ID, and iOS data protection). You are responsible for securing your device and any backups or exports you create. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Children

CrewWho is a professional tool intended for adults and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction).

Your rights (California / EEA / UK and others)

Because we do not collect or hold your personal information on our systems, most data-subject requests are satisfied directly on your device (you can access, correct, export, or delete your data in the app). We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA, and we do not use it for targeted advertising. For third-party services you enable, exercise your rights with those providers directly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new “Last updated” date; continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.
